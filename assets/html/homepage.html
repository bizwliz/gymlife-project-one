Who are we?

We are a dynamic team of four students with a shared passion for innovation and technology. Our names are Lissette Garcia, Mia Olmedillo, Jusu Karneh, and Alex Delva - the creative minds behind the app "GYMPAL". Driven by our common interest in fitness, we embarked on a journey to channel our diverse skillsets into something that can be used by anyone and everyone. Together, we fused our backgrounds in Java, HTML, & CSS to build an application that has the potential to redefine inclusivity in the fitness community.

The Importance Of A Balanced Lifestyle

Here at GYMPAL, our ultimate mission is to empower individuals to lead healthier and happier lives by providing invaluable resources and unwavering support in achieving a balanced lifestyle. We take great pride in our commitment to inclusivity, ensuring that we cater to diverse dietary restrictions, unique needs, and varying knowledge levels across different exercise domains. Our goal is to make your journey seamless and enjoyable.
